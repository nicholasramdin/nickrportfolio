Video Game Nexus


Video Game Nexus is a Video Game forum created by yours truly. It was my first website that I ever developed. I originally created it for my first college course in web design. At first it started as a really barren, basic website. With text, images and overall content all over the place.


But soon after, it became something I was passionate about developing on my free time. Throughout the experience I gained through my web design courses as well as from my own curiousity at home, the website has became pretty fleshed out with intricate styles of design and is much more appealing to the eye than it once was. Of course, I am steadily updating it whenever I learn something new. Therefore, expect the sample images below to change every now and then since I will update it here as well. My goal is to establish it as a full fledged Video Game forum once I feel it is ready for the world.
